Microsoft365.com/setup



Microsoft 365 previously known as office 365 is similar to MS office suite but is the option of a subscription. Microsoft 365 provides you with all the applications of Microsoft office, including MS Word, MS Access and more. Microsoft365.com/setup allows you to download and activate the Microsoft Office setup. If you have a Microsoft account, sign in and enter the product key at Microsoft365.com/setup to activate your product. Install and set up your Office 365 apps with the given instructions.


Steps to Set Up Your Microsoft 365 Subscription

  • Step 1: Create a Microsoft Account
    • Go to Microsoft365.com/setup
    • Click "Create a Microsoft account"
    • Enter your email address, password, and other details
    • Verify your account through the confirmation email
  • Step 2: Redeem Your Product Key
    • Go to (link unavailable)
    • Sign in with your Microsoft account
    • Enter your 25-character product key
    • Click "Next" to redeem your key
  • Step 3: Choose Your Subscription Plan
    • Select your subscription plan (Personal, Home, Business, etc.)
    • Review the plan details and pricing
    • Click "Next" to proceed
  • Step 4: Download and Install Microsoft 365
    • Click "Download" to download the Microsoft 365 installer
    • Run the installer and follow the prompts to install
  • Step 5: Activate Your Subscription
    • Launch any Microsoft 365 application
    • Sign in with your Microsoft account
    • Enter your product key (if prompted)
    • Activate your subscription
  • Step 6: Set Up Your Account
    • Configure your account settings and preferences
    • Set up OneDrive cloud storage and other services
    • Explore the Microsoft 365 software and start using the applications!

Download and Install Microsoft 365 using product key

  • Go to "Microsoft365.com/setup".
  • Sign in with your Microsoft account or create a new one.
  • Enter your product key (if you have one).
  • Select your subscription plan (Personal, Home, Business, etc.).
  • Click "Download" to download the Microsoft 365 installer.
  • Run the downloaded installer file (usually named "Microsoft365Setup.exe").
  • Click "Yes" to allow the program to make changes to your device.
  • Select your language and click "Next".
  • Choose whether to install the 32-bit or 64-bit version (depending on your device).
  • Click "Next" to begin the installation process.
  • Wait for the installation to complete (this may take a few minutes).
